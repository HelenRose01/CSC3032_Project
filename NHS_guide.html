Order repeat prescriptions
At the bottom of the screen find this button:
When this button is pressed, it will take you to this screen:
On this screen, there is a button which takes you to a page which shows your orders and their status. There is
also a button which takes you to a page where you can see and change which pharmacy your prescriptions are
sent to. To order a prescription, as we are doing in this guide, press the green button which says 'order a
prescription' which will take you to this screen:
Press the circle next to 'A repeat prescription' and then press 'continue'.
If this is the pharmacy you want the prescription to be sent to press 'continue' (if it is not the pharmacy you want
the prescription sent to click the blue link which says 'Change your nominated pharmacy'). Pressing 'continue'
will send you to a page like this:
Press the squares next to the medicine(s) you want to order. If you want to send a message to the GP with the order
then press the rectangle and type out your message. Press continue and then the order will be sent.
